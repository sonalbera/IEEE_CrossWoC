    What is CrossWoC ?
    CrossWoC is a six-week long opensource event organised by IEEE DTU & IEEE DTU CS, which gives programmers and innovators an opportunity to bring out their nascent talent and find intriguing solutions to real-world problems. It provides a platform for developers to dig deeper into their gray matter and bring out their latent creativity through open source.

    This is the first edition of CrossWoC conducted by IEEE DTU & IEEE DTU CS, and it will be held from February, 2020, to 28th March, 2021.

    FAQ 🤔

    The CrossWoC is a 6-week open-source development period partnered with IEEE DTU which give programmers and innovators an opportunity to bring out their nascent talent and find intriguing solutions to real-world problems. endl;

    The event is a great learning opportunity for both experienced programmers as well as newbies because it takes a deep dive into the world of open-source programming.

    The maximum age of the participant must be 25. They must not necessarily be a college student.

    13th February to 28th March, 2021

    Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Absolutely free. Admission to CrossWoC is completely free, all thanks to our sponsors!

    This event will require individual participation. However, you can request help from mentors & fellow participants.

    A valid government issued photo ID and/or your college issued photo ID, laptop, phone, chargers, a GitHub account & and the enthusiasm to learn.

    The companies will provide their juries who will judge the projects in the respective fields based on the development of the project during the 6 weeks of the event.

    It will be an online only event.
